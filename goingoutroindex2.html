Kiley's Story

I had the idea for Outro after two of my friends were drugged in the same month. After talking to more of my friends and hearing their stories, I decided something had to be done. Going out had gotten scary. I was frustrated and confused that there wasn’t a platform for girls, LBGTQ+ community members or people of color to find spaces where they can feel safe AND have fun so I set out to find a solution.
